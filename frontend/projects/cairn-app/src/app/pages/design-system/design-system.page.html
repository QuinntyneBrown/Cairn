Why the rule exists

Toronto yellow as text on white is 1.13:1. It is below, rendered at the size a heading would use. If you can read it, the monitor is lying.

This is #FFF737 text on white — 1.13:1

Same trap, less obvious: white on --positive is 3.23:1 and fails AA for body text. Hence one dark text colour on every fill, without exception.
